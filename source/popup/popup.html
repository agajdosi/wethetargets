About

Each of us is the target of hundreds of targeted ads every day without any insight into the complex processes behind them. This extension collects these targeted ads and speeches from digital platforms and shares them with other users of this extension in order to reveal how, what and whom the digital platforms and advertisers are targeting.

Demographic information

We need to know basic demographic information about you so we can match the ads targeted at you with your demographic group. Please, fill the information you are comfortable sharing with us. All the information is anonymous and will be used only for research purposes.
